# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, potential employers, and collaborators looking for a Security Analyst.

## Product Purpose
A personal portfolio website for Kritsana Rakaying to showcase skills, experience, and contact information.

## Brand Personality
Cyberpunk, Hacker, Glitchy, Playful (Pixel/Minecraft inspired).

## Anti-references
Generic corporate templates, overly clean/minimalist SaaS designs without character.

## Design Principles
- Embrace the hacker aesthetic with terminal-like text and matrix effects.
- Use pixel/8-bit elements sparingly but effectively for headers.
- Motion should be glitchy and dimensional (Nether portal, floating elements).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Maintain high contrast against the dark background. Ensure animations don't prevent reading.
